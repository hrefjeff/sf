Closing Notes

  • His jHK is one of the best in the game and very difficult to anti-air
  • From mid-range they will often keep jumping with jHK to approach
  • You want to stand right at the range of his crLP, then you’ll be able to anti-air
  • Dogura vs Itazan from the RAGE league is an example of how to play this
  • Dashing forward is effective. Even if you eat stHP, it won’t be a crush counter so it’s not that painful
  • The aim will be to V-Reversal just 1 time on guaranteed V-Reversalable string so instead, go for a throw
  • His still is 1000, which doesn’t match his 1075 health
  • His command grab range got nerfed
  • Overall, still a relatively strong character
  • If he’s running towards you, IT’S BETTER TO JUMP FORWARD
